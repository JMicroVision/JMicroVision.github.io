Academic Citation

If you use JMicroVision in your research or publications, please cite it using the following format:

Required Elements:

  • Author: Nicolas Roduit
  • Title: JMicroVision: Image analysis toolbox for measuring and quantifying components of high-definition images
  • Version number (e.g., 1.3.5)
  • URL: https://jmicrovision.github.io/
  • Access date

Example Citation:

Roduit, N. (2025). JMicroVision: Image analysis toolbox for measuring and quantifying components of high-definition images. Version 1.3.5. https://jmicrovision.github.io/ (accessed 30 December 2025).
